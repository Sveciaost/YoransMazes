Maze Format

Maze levels can be stored in a plain text file, much like the SOK format.

Any line which begins with a semi-colon (;) is a comment. These lines are completely ignored by the parser.

Header

The file must begin with the title of the levelpack and the author. One or more lines after the header must be completely blank.

; This is a comment
Title: SokoMaze
Author: Yoran

Levels

Levels must be separated by completely blank lines. The level format is the same as the level format for Sokoban, with some additions. Unlike Sokoban's level format however, levels do not have to be surrounded by walls (#).

Sokoban Level Format

Level ElementCharacter
Wall#
Player@
Player on Goal Square (sensor)+
Box$
Box on Goal Square (sensor)*
Goal Square (sensor).
Floor [SPACE]

Maze Additions

Toxic Waste

The player cannot cross toxic waste unless a box is pushed into it.

Level ElementCharacter
Toxic Waste~
Block in Water`
Player on a block in water^
Block on top of a block!

Goal Door

If there's no goal door, the level will end when all the sensors (Goal Squares) have boxes on them.

Level ElementCharacter
Goal Door at TopH
Goal Door at RightI
Goal Door at BottomJ
Goal Door at LeftK

Decoration

Decorations aren't needed, but can make the levels look less bland.

Tables stretch across two spaces. Put a "%" (decoration floor - floor the player cannot walk on) below a vertical table and to the right of a horizontal table.

Level ElementCharacter(s)
Decoration Floor (Used for tables)%
Horizontal TablesZ,Y and X
Vertical TablesW, V and U
Barrels1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7
Concrete Pole:
Horizontal Wall-
Vertical Wall?
